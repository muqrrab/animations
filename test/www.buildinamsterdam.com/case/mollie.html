An IDENTITY BUILT FOR GROWTH

Mollie is all about simplifying complex financial services, which leads to growth of companies of all sizes. We developed their brand narrative and identity around this core concept.

expressing the mollie brand

We applied the identity to a wide range of on and offline touchpoints. Next to basic stationery, we designed unique employee merchandise, various campaigns, and signage in their HQ.

MORE THAN JUST GRAPHIC ELEMENTS

To visualize Mollie’s takes on simplifying complex financial services, we use basic shapes: a circle & a square, that converge and act dynamically to conceive transparency.

MERCHANDISE

We are constantly extending Mollie’s identity with various applications. For Mollie merch, we designed multiple items and packaging, including these unique patches to celebrate the anniversaries of Mollie employees.

mollie in motion

We defined the concept of Mollie’s motion design by constructing a clear hierarchy of text, colors and graphic elements. allowing us to create a visual system with strong brand recognition.

motion design

We defined the core concept of Mollie’s motion design by constructiong the clear hierarchy of text, colors and graphic elements. Allowing us to create a visual system that expresses Mollie’s brand values of being loved, bold, and authentic,

A DYNAMIC SYSTEM

The visual identity is done in such a way that it can be extended and morphed into new shapes while still being highly recognizable.

mollie.com

We designed Mollie’s future .com based on the identity, without transforming towards a completely new set up.

scalable design system

We set up an online design system and content guideline for Mollie’s design team in a way that they can build various blocks while still staying true to their brand values.

online content guideline

From displaying product overview to illustrating features or simply creating clear communication, we help Mollie with creating templates and guidance for their online content creation.

brand guideline

We set up an in-depth brand book for Mollie’s internal team where we extensively explain all do’s and don'ts around their brand. We defined the core elements that will distinct every Mollie campaigns from other brands.
